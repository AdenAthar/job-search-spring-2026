# Career Your Adventure – Job Application Campaign

## What I Did

For this adventure I ran a structured job application campaign targeting entry-level software engineering and machine learning roles. Over the course of the assignment I researched companies, tailored application materials, and submitted over 20 applications across a range of companies and role types.

## Career Goals

Short-term, I'm looking to land a full-time role in either software engineering or ML/AI engineering after graduating in May 2026. I'm intentionally keeping both tracks open because my background spans general SWE work and applied machine learning, and I want to see where I get traction. Long-term, I'd like to move into a more specialized ML engineering or research-adjacent role, potentially alongside or after a graduate degree depending on how things shake out.

## Deliverable Summary

The main deliverable is an application tracker spreadsheet that logs each company, role title, location, salary info where available, date applied, and a direct link to the job posting. This documents the actual effort put in and gives a clear picture of the scope and targeting strategy behind the search.

In addition to the tracker, this repo includes the resume and cover letter templates used throughout the process, along with tailored versions where applicable.

## Repo Contents

- `tracker.xlsx` – full application log with 20+ entries
- `resume.pdf` – resume used for applications
- `cover_letter_template.md` – base cover letter with notes on how it was tailored per role
- `reflection.md` – written reflection on the experience
