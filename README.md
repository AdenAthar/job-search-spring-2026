# Career Your Adventure – Job Application Campaign

## What I Did

For this assignment I ran a structured job search targeting entry-level software
engineering and machine learning roles. Over the course of the assignment I
researched companies, tailored application materials, and submitted 25+
applications across a range of companies and role types — from large companies
like Microsoft, NVIDIA, and Salesforce to smaller ML-focused startups.

## Career Goals

Short-term, I'm looking to land a full-time role in either software engineering
or ML/AI engineering after graduating in May 2026. I'm keeping both tracks open
because my background spans general SWE work and applied ML research, and I want
to see where I get traction. Long-term, I'd like to move into a more specialized
ML engineering or research-adjacent role.

## Deliverable Summary

The main deliverable is a job application tracker (screenshot included) logging
each company, role title, location, salary info where available, status, and a
direct link to the job posting. This documents the scope and targeting strategy
behind the search across 25+ applications.
