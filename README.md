# Career Your Adventure – Job Application Campaign

## What I Did

For this adventure I ran a structured job application campaign targeting entry-level software engineering and machine learning roles. Over the course of the assignment I researched companies, tailored application materials, and submitted **30+ applications** across a wide range of companies and role types — from large tech companies to consulting firms to startups.

## Career Goals

Short-term, I'm looking to land a full-time role in either software engineering or ML/AI engineering after graduating in May 2026. I'm intentionally keeping both tracks open because my background spans general SWE work and applied machine learning, and I want to see where I get traction. Long-term, I'd like to move into a more specialized ML engineering or research-adjacent role, potentially alongside or after a graduate degree depending on how things shake out.

I'm also navigating STEM OPT authorization (starting July 2026), so H-1B sponsorship strength is a real factor in how I've prioritized companies.

## Deliverable Summary

The main deliverable is an application tracker documenting each company, role title, location, date applied, and current status. This documents the actual effort put in and gives a clear picture of the scope and targeting strategy behind the search.

In addition to the tracker, this repo includes the resume used throughout the process, screenshots of the application pipeline at key points in time, and a reflection on the full experience.
